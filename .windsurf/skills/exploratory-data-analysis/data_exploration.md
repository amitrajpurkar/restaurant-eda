# SKILL: Data Exploration (EDA)

## Purpose
Perform structured, reproducible exploratory data analysis.

## Principles
- Do not jump to modeling
- Surface data quality issues early
- Prefer summary statistics over intuition

## Steps
1. Inspect schema, data types, and shape
2. Identify missing values and outliers
3. Analyze distributions and skew
4. Check correlations and multicollinearity
5. Identify potential data leakage

## Output Expectations
- Summary tables
- Key observations
- Risks or anomalies
- Suggested next steps

## Constraints
- Do not mutate original data
- Do not assume domain meaning without evidence
